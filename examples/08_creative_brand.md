## 1. Brand Diagnosis

### Heritage positioning built on price no longer resonates — brand relevance fell 34 points in 3 years
**Situation:** Brand has 94% unaided awareness among 35–54 cohort but only 31% among 18–34; price positioning drove $4.2B revenue for 20 years but Gen Z values brand story over price point
**Complication:** Competitor Shein owns price signal; Target owns value-plus-style; no distinct territory available for pure price play — brand is in no man's land with 18–34 demographic
**Resolution:** Repositioning to circular fashion leadership — resale, repair, and rental — creates defensible, values-led territory that drives 40% premium pricing power and 2.8× higher Gen Z loyalty scores
> Source: Brand tracking study n=4,200; Nielsen Gen Z Values Report 2025; Internal customer cohort analysis

### Gen Z rejects price positioning but actively seeks brand authenticity signals
- 78% of Gen Z shoppers research brand sustainability credentials before first purchase
- 64% willing to pay 30%+ premium for verifiable circular credentials vs 18% of Boomers
- Brand loyalty 2.8× higher when brand values align with personal identity, vs price-matched brand
- Social proof now drives 67% of Gen Z discovery — peer-to-peer beats paid media 4:1
> Source: Edelman Trust Barometer 2025; First Insight Gen Z Report; Internal brand research

> — Coco Chanel

### Circular fashion leadership is unowned, values-aligned, and commercially proven

### Three-pillar circular strategy transforms brand identity with existing infrastructure
#### Pillar 1: Resale
- Branded resale marketplace launches Q2 2026
- In-store drop-off points in all 340 locations
- Resale drives 22% incremental revenue from existing inventory lifecycle
#### Pillar 2: Repair
- Free lifetime repair programme for own-brand items
- In-store repair hubs create destination visits and brand storytelling moments
- Repair NPS 94 — highest brand touchpoint in pilot testing
#### Pillar 3: Rental
- Occasion-wear rental subscription: $39/month, 4 items
- Pilot in 12 cities shows 3.4× higher lifetime value vs single-purchase customer
