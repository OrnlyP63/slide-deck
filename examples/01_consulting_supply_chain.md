## 1. Situation

### Global supply chains face unprecedented concentration risk after 2020–2024 disruptions
**Situation:** 78% of Fortune 500 manufacturers rely on a single region for >60% of critical inputs; pre-COVID average was 45%
**Complication:** Three compounding shocks — pandemic, Red Sea conflict, Taiwan Strait tensions — exposed structural fragility costing $4.2T globally in 2020–2023
**Resolution:** Dual-source diversification with regional buffer inventory reduces disruption exposure by 40% at 8–12% cost premium, delivering positive ROI within 18 months
> Source: McKinsey Global Institute 2024; World Bank Supply Chain Monitor; Company analysis

### Single-source concentration has tripled since 2005 driven by cost optimisation
- 73% of tier-1 supplier relationships are single-source, up from 24% in 2005
- Cost-driven offshoring compressed margins but eliminated redundancy
- Just-in-time inventory left zero buffer for demand or supply shocks
- Insurance and resilience investment declined 60% in real terms since 2010
> Source: Resilinc Supply Chain Risk Report 2024; Deloitte Manufacturing Survey

### Three scenarios each alone could halt production for 60–90 days
#### Scenario A: Geopolitical Shock
- Taiwan Strait disruption cuts 85% of advanced semiconductor supply
- 67-day average lead time to qualify alternate supplier
- $340M revenue at risk per production halt month
#### Scenario B: Climate Event
- Typhoon season disrupts Southeast Asian chemical supply 3.2× per decade
- Single facility concentration means no fallback
- $180M inventory buffer required under current design

## 3. Resolution

### Dual-source strategy with regional buffers delivers 40% risk reduction at 8% cost premium
- Qualify second-source supplier for all 14 critical inputs within 12 months
- Establish regional buffer inventory: 45-day cover in EU, North America, Southeast Asia
- Digital supply chain visibility platform monitors concentration in real time
- Annual resilience audit replaces one-time risk assessment
> Source: Bain Supply Chain Resilience Benchmark 2024; Internal financial model

### Implementation roadmap delivers quick wins in 90 days and full resilience in 18 months
1. Q1 2026: Qualify alternates for top 3 inputs; deploy visibility dashboard
2. Q2–Q3 2026: Complete dual-source qualification for all 14 inputs; buffer warehousing
3. Q4 2026: Regional buffers operational; resilience SLAs signed with all tier-1 suppliers
> Source: Internal project plan; Procurement team sign-off Q4 2025
