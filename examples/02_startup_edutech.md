## 1. The Problem

### 87 million workers face displacement by 2030 with no affordable reskilling path
**Situation:** Automation eliminates 87M jobs by 2030; 65% are blue-collar roles requiring hands-on vocational reskilling — not university degrees
**Complication:** Traditional vocational training costs $8,000–$25,000 and takes 12–24 months; employer-sponsored programmes reach only 12% of at-risk workers
**Resolution:** SkillBridge delivers employer-validated micro-credentials in 8–12 weeks at $400/learner through AI-personalised simulation and on-demand mentorship
> Source: WEF Future of Jobs Report 2025; OECD Skills Outlook 2024; Company research
